## User Manual and Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the KSRCE AI Lab Management Platform! This guide will walk you through the essential features of the platform and help you get started with using the AI Lab.

### 1. Creating Your Account

To start using the platform, you first need to create an account.

1.  **Navigate to the Signup Page**: Open your web browser and go to the platform's homepage. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
2.  **Fill in Your Details**: Enter your name, email address, and a secure password. 
3.  **Verify Your Email**: You will receive an email with a One-Time Password (OTP). Enter this OTP on the verification page to complete your registration.

### 2. Logging In

Once your account is created and verified, you can log in to the platform.

1.  **Go to the Login Page**: Click on the "Login" button on the homepage.
2.  **Enter Your Credentials**: Use the email and password you registered with.
3.  **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: For enhanced security, you will be prompted to enter a new OTP sent to your email each time you log in.

### 3. Your Dashboard

After logging in, you will be taken to your personal dashboard. The dashboard provides an overview of your account and quick access to the platform's main features.

- **Wallet Balance**: Displays your current wallet balance.
- **Recent Bookings**: Shows a list of your recent GPU slot and mentor session bookings.
- **Quick Actions**: Buttons to quickly book a new slot or add funds to your wallet.

### 4. Managing Your Wallet

To book GPU slots or mentor sessions, you need to have funds in your wallet.

1.  **Go to the Wallet Page**: From your dashboard, click on the "Wallet" or "Add Funds" button.
2.  **Enter Recharge Amount**: Specify the amount you wish to add to your wallet.
3.  **Complete Payment**: You will be redirected to our secure payment gateway (Razorpay) to complete the transaction. You can use various payment methods like credit/debit cards, UPI, or net banking.
4.  **Confirmation**: Once the payment is successful, the amount will be instantly credited to your wallet.

### 5. Booking a GPU Slot

You can book high-performance GPU slots for your AI/ML projects.

1.  **Navigate to the Booking Page**: From the dashboard, click on "Book a Slot".
2.  **Select a Slot**: You will see a calendar view of available GPU slots. Choose a date and time that suits you.
3.  **Confirm Booking**: Review the booking details, including the duration and cost. The cost will be automatically deducted from your wallet balance.
4.  **Access Your Slot**: Once booked, you will receive instructions on how to access your dedicated GPU environment.

### 6. Booking a Mentor Session

Get guidance from experienced mentors for your projects.

1.  **Go to the Mentors Page**: Find the "Mentors" section on the platform.
2.  **Choose a Mentor**: Browse through the list of available mentors and view their profiles, expertise, and hourly rates.
3.  **Book a Session**: Select a mentor and choose an available time slot for your session.
4.  **Payment**: The session fee will be deducted from your wallet.

### 7. Troubleshooting

- **Login Issues**: If you are unable to log in, try resetting your password. If the problem persists, contact support.
- **Payment Failures**: If a payment fails, please check your payment details and try again. If the issue continues, contact our support team with the transaction details.
- **Slot Access Problems**: If you have trouble accessing your booked GPU slot, please refer to the access instructions provided or contact technical support immediately.

We hope this guide helps you get the most out of the KSRCE AI Lab Management Platform. Happy learning and innovating!
